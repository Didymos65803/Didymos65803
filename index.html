About Me

I am an undergraduate student at National Taiwan University with broad research interests, primarily focused on astronomical data analysis.

As a Founder of Hoshiko Plan (NGO for STEM education) and member of astronomy club, I am passionate about science communication.

Lighting up the starry sky in the dark of night.

Education

  • National Taiwan University, Department of Physics (2022 - Present)
  • National Hsinchu Senior High School (2019 - 2022)

Research Projects

Inferring Obscured Cosmic Black Hole Accretion History

Using data from the JWST/MIRI CEERS Survey to study black hole accretion history of obscured active galactic nuclei (AGNs).

Identifying Binary Asteroids Using Machine Learning

Developing machine learning approaches to identify binary asteroids from lightcurve data in the FOSSIL survey.

Atmospheric Dynamics on Venus

Utilizing a General Circulation Model to investigate mechanisms driving periodic oscillations in Venus.
